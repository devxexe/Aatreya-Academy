Our Code Of Conduct

Setting the foundation for discipline, integrity, and mutual respect within our community.

/01

Punctuality

Time is our most valuable resource. We maintain strict adherence to schedules to foster a culture of readiness and respect.

/02

Digital Ethics

Technology is a tool for empowerment. We ensure all digital interactions are purposeful, safe, and ethically grounded.

/03

Mutual Respect

Every individual is a pillar of our institution. We uphold a zero-tolerance policy for bullying or exclusion in any form.

/04

Integrity

Honesty in academics and character is non-negotiable. We take pride in our work and responsibility for our actions.

/05

Campus Pride

We treat our campus and resources with the utmost care, reflecting our collective commitment to excellence.

/06

Global Citizenship

We prepare students to be compassionate leaders who contribute positively to the local and global community.
